5. Bookings and Payments

5.1 Booking Process

  1. Student selects tutor and requests session
  2. Teacher accepts or declines request
  3. Deposit required (20% of session total)
  4. Deposit verified by admin
  5. Booking confirmed
  6. Session occurs
  7. Remaining balance paid to teacher

5.2 Pricing

  • Teachers set their own hourly rates
  • Tutorly service fee: 20% added to student's total
  • Students pay: (Teacher Rate × 1.20) × Hours
  • Teachers receive: Teacher Rate × Hours
  • Platform earns: Teacher Rate × Hours × 0.20

5.3 Payment Methods

  • Deposit: InstaPay only (proof upload required)
  • Remaining balance: InstaPay or cash (at teacher's discretion)
  • No refunds on platform fees

5.4 Deposit Requirement

  • All bookings require a 20% deposit
  • Deposit must be paid within 12 hours of teacher acceptance
  • Unverified deposits result in booking expiration
