Privacy Policy for Tutorly

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Tutorly ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the safety of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Tutorly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you create an account:

For All Users:

  • Email address
  • Full name
  • Date of birth (for age verification and parental consent)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Location (city and area)

For Teachers:

  • Teaching credentials and experience
  • Subjects and grade levels taught
  • Hourly rates
  • InstaPay account information (for payments)
  • Background check information (verification status only)

For Students/Parents:

  • Student grade level
  • School name
  • Subjects needing help
  • Booker type (parent or student)
  • Parent/guardian email (for users under 18)

1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use Tutorly:

  • Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage data (features used, time spent, interactions)
  • Log data (IP address, access times, app crashes)

1.3 Location Data

With your permission, we collect location data to:

  • Show nearby tutors
  • Coordinate in-person sessions
  • Your exact address is only shared after booking confirmation

1.4 Camera and Photo Library

With your permission, we access:

  • Camera: To take profile photos and share session materials
  • Photo library: To select profile pictures, upload documents, and submit payment proof

1.5 Payment Information

We do not store payment card information. All payments are processed through InstaPay, a third-party payment service. We only store:

  • Payment proof screenshots (uploaded by users)
  • Transaction references
  • Payment status

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Services

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Match students with appropriate tutors
  • Facilitate booking and scheduling
  • Process payments and deposits
  • Enable in-app messaging between users

2.2 Safety and Verification

  • Verify age and parental consent for users under 18
  • Conduct background checks for teachers (when provided)
  • Moderate user-generated content (reviews, messages)
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

2.3 Communication

  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Notify users of session changes
  • Respond to support requests
  • Send important policy updates

2.4 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve features
  • Conduct research and development
  • Generate aggregated, anonymized statistics

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited circumstances:

3.1 Between Users

  • Teacher profiles are visible to all users (name, photo, subjects, ratings, reviews, location)
  • Student names in reviews appear as "Verified Student" to protect privacy
  • Exact addresses for in-person sessions are shared only after booking confirmation

3.2 Service Providers

  • InstaPay (payment processing)
  • Cloud hosting providers (data storage)
  • Analytics services (anonymized usage data)

3.3 Legal Requirements

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activity

3.4 Business Transfers

  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

4. Child Safety and Parental Consent

4.1 Age Requirements

  • Minimum age to use Tutorly: 13 years old
  • Users under 18 require parental consent

4.2 Parental Controls

  • Parents can monitor their child's bookings and activity
  • Parent/guardian email required for users under 18
  • Parents can request account deletion at any time

4.3 Safety Features

  • In-app messaging only (no external contact sharing)
  • Background check verification badges for teachers
  • Location sharing restricted until booking confirmation
  • Content moderation for reviews and messages

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited employee access to personal data
  • Secure payment processing through third parties
However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Update

  • View and edit your profile information at any time
  • Download a copy of your data

6.2 Deletion

  • Request account deletion by contacting support
  • Some information may be retained for legal/safety purposes

6.3 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional emails (service emails still required)

6.4 Location Permissions

  • Disable location access in device settings (may limit functionality)

7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • Active accounts: For as long as your account exists
  • Deleted accounts: Up to 90 days for backup/recovery
  • Legal requirements: As required by law
  • Reviews and ratings: Retained to maintain platform integrity

8. Third-Party Services

Tutorly uses third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

InstaPay (Payment Processing)

  • Processes all payment transactions
  • Has separate privacy policy and terms
  • We do not store payment card information

Cloud Storage

  • Stores app data and user-generated content
  • Subject to provider's security standards

9. International Users

Tutorly is designed for users in Egypt. If you access our services from outside Egypt, your information may be transferred to and processed in Egypt.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • In-app notification
  • Email notification
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
